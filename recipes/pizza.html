Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 cup tomato paste , unsalted
  • 1 tsp salt (Note 1)
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp garlic , finely minced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1/2 tsp dried parsley

Preparation

  1. Use 1/4 cup of pizza sauce for each pizza (30cm/12" wide)
  2. Place raw pizza crust on a pizza pan (preferably with holes). Spread sauce evenly using the back of a spoon, leaving a 1cm / 1/2" border for the crust
  3. Cover with toppings of choice (less is more!). Standard pizza – sprinkle with 130g / 4.5 oz freshly shredded mozzarella cheese, then 1 cup toppings such as sliced pepperoni
  4. Bake in preheated 275°C / 530°F oven on top shelf for 10 minutes, rotating at 4 minutes, until cheese has golden specks and crust is crisp
