Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 9–12 lasagna noodles (cooked al dente)
  • Meat sauce: 1 lb ground beef, 1 lb Italian sausage, onions, garlic, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and herbs like oregano and basil
  • Cheese Filling: 15 oz ricotta cheese, 1 egg, 1/3 cup Parmesan, and 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • Topping: 1 lb shredded mozzarella cheese and additional ParmesanPasta: 9–12 lasagna noodles (cooked al dente)

Preparation

  1. Brown the meat: Cook ground beef and sausage with onions and garlic, then stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings. Simmer for 30 minutes
  2. Mix the cheese: Combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, and parsley in a bowl
  3. Layer: In a 9x13 baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce, then noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and more meat sauce. Repeat the layers three times, finishing with sauce and mozzarella
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and browned
  5. Rest: Let the lasagna sit for at least 15 minutes before slicing to allow it to set
